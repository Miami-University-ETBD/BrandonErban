Quick Tips for Plant Care

  • Water regularly: Most plants need water every 1-2 weeks, depending on their size.
  • Place in the right light: Ensure your plant gets the right amount of sunlight. Some need bright light, while others thrive in low light.
  • Use good soil: Healthy soil is key to plant growth. Make sure to use nutrient-rich soil for your plants.
